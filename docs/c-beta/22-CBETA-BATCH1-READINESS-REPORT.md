# Cβ Batch 1 Readiness Report

## Selection Strategy

Adopted strategy: role-differentiation first, filtered by student demand and research feasibility.

Batch 1 intentionally avoids five similar communication roles. It covers five different work kernels:

- technical defect diagnosis
- IT solution qualification
- hiring-process diagnosis
- advertising metric experimentation
- public-sector emergency preparedness

## Selected Roles

| production order | industry | roleSlug | role | reason |
|---:|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | `it` | `web-engineer` | Webエンジニア/バックエンド | student demand is high and existing Cα scenario can be converted carefully |
| 2 | `marketing-media` | `performance-marketing` | 運用型広告担当 | metric-heavy and useful for anti-generic calibration |
| 3 | `it` | `it-solution-sales` | ITソリューション営業 | sales pipeline role that must not become generic sales |
| 4 | `hr` | `recruitment-consultant` | 採用コンサルタント | hiring fairness and funnel diagnosis create distinct materials |
| 5 | `public-infra` | `disaster-crisis-management` | 防災/危機管理担当 | public-sector constraints and uncertainty stress the quality gate |

## Why These Roles Calibrate Cβ

- They are easy for students to recognize but easy to misunderstand.
- They use different artifacts: logs, campaign reports, business workflows, hiring funnels, hazard maps.
- They stress different metrics: error rate, CPA, ROI, pass rate, shelter capacity.
- They reveal whether the quality gate can prevent generic stakeholder-coordination patterns.

## Expected Risks

- `web-engineer`: may remain too close to existing Cα task UI unless rewritten as one continuous 5-scene day.
- `it-solution-sales`: may collapse into polite customer handling unless IT requirements and implementation constraints lead each decision.
- `recruitment-consultant`: may become generic candidate support unless funnel and evaluation artifacts drive the scenes.
- `performance-marketing`: may become generic marketing planning unless daily metrics and experiment design are explicit.
- `disaster-crisis-management`: may become generic public communication unless hazard and capacity materials drive choices.

## Acceptance Criteria for Step 3

- Generate exactly 5 full Cβ scenarios for the selected roles.
- Each scenario is immersive-only and exactly 5 scenes / 5 tasks.
- Each scenario has structured `roleWorkKernel`.
- Each scene has `workMaterial` and `roleSpecificity.kernelConnection`.
- Every choice has realistic good/risk/immediate/delayed/next/result feedback, `meterEffects`, and `misconceptionEffect`.
- Update the matrix from `batch1_selected_ready_for_production` to Cβ-ready only after quality gates pass.
- Do not proceed to Batch 2 if warnings show generic stakeholder-coordination drift.
