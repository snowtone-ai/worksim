# HR Scenario Creation Guide

## Purpose

This guide is for HR or business-side staff who want to draft an original WorkSim scenario without editing JSON.

The goal is not to reproduce a whole job perfectly. The goal is to extract one realistic workday where a student can understand:

- what information this role reads
- what trade-offs this role faces
- what output this role is expected to produce
- what misconception about the job should be corrected

## Recommended Flow

1. Choose one role and one realistic day.
2. Write five tasks only.
3. For each task, write the material the student must inspect.
4. Create three choices that are all plausible.
5. Explain what each choice reveals about the student's decision style.
6. Remove confidential names, customer names, and internal terms.

## Good Scenario

- Uses a concrete work material: proposal memo, demand forecast, claim log, interview notes, operation schedule.
- Shows a real constraint: deadline, budget, risk, compliance, customer impact, staffing.
- Offers three plausible choices.
- Uses student-friendly words.
- Makes the hidden reality of the job visible.

## Weak Scenario

- Only says "communicate with stakeholders."
- Has one obviously correct choice and two joke choices.
- Requires confidential company knowledge.
- Uses unexplained abbreviations.
- Teaches company PR instead of job reality.

## Writing Rule

Use this shape for every task:

```text
You are in role X.
You are looking at material Y.
The situation is Z.
You must decide A, B, or C.
Each choice changes what the student learns about the role.
```

## Safety Rule

Do not include:

- real customer names
- personal information
- confidential numbers
- unreleased products
- employee names
- discriminatory expressions
- screening or hiring evaluation wording aimed at identifying an individual student

Use fictional company, product, and customer names unless a legal review has approved real names.
